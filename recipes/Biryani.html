Chicken Biryani Recipe

Description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic Pakistani rice dish made with marinated chicken, basmati rice, and a blend of spices. Itâ€™s known for its rich taste and vibrant colors, often enhanced with saffron and garnished with fried onions and fresh herbs.

Time Needed :1:30 hour

Ingredients Needed

  • 500g chicken pieces (bone-in or boneless)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala (store-bought or homemade)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil

Steps to Follow

  1. Marinate Chicken: Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, and lemon juice. Coat chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook Rice: Rinse and soak basmati rice, then cook in boiling water with cardamom, cloves, bay leaf, cumin seeds, and salt until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare Biryani: Heat ghee or oil, fry onions until golden brown, then add green chilies and tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes soften. Add marinated chicken, cook until done, and mix in biryani masala.
  4. Layer and Cook: Layer partially cooked rice over the chicken. Sprinkle cilantro, mint, and saffron milk (if using) on top. Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Serve: Fluff the biryani, garnish with fried onions, and serve hot with raita or salad.
