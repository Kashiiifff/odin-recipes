Aloo Ghoobi Recipe

Aloo Gobi is a classic Pakistani dish made with potatoes (aloo) and cauliflower (gobi) cooked with aromatic spices. It's a flavorful and comforting dish that's perfect as a side or a main course, typically enjoyed with rice or roti

Time Needed :45 mins

Ingredients Needed

  • 500g potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 500g cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin-coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)

Steps to Follow

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
  2. Add chopped onion and ginger-garlic paste. Cook until onion is golden brown.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Stir in turmeric, cumin-coriander powder, and garam masala. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add potatoes and cauliflower. Mix well, cover, and cook on low heat until vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and garnish with cilantro.
