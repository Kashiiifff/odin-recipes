Chicken Karachi Recipe

Description

This dish is the most commanly popular dish of Pakistan Below we will discuss how can we make this recipes as easiest we can.

Time Needed :1 Hour

Ingredients needed

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces.
  • 2tbsp oil.
  • 1 onion, choped.
  • 2 tomatoes, choped.
  • 2 green chilies, sliced.
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste.
  • 1 tsp red chili powder.
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder.
  • 1 tsp garam masala.
  • Salt to taste.
  • Fresh coriander, chopped (for garnish).

Steps you need to follow

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Saut√© onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for 1 min.
  3. Add chicken, cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomatoes, green chilies, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Cook until chicken is done.
  5. Add garam masala and cook for 2 more minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Enjoy your Chiken Karahi Mates :D
