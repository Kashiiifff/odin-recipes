Aloo Channa Recipe

Aloo Channa is a popular Pakistani breakfast dish made with potatoes and chickpeas. It's a hearty and flavorful start to the day, often enjoyed with naan or paratha.

Time Needed :45 mins

Ingredients Needed

  • 2 large potatoes, cubed
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin-coriander powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped cilantro

Steps to Follow

  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, and saut√© onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and spices. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  3. Add potatoes and chickpeas. Mix, cover, and cook until potatoes are tender.
  4. Season with salt and garnish with cilantro.

Make breakfast lit with this Aloo Channa!
